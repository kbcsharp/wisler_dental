Extraction Services

If our staff determines that you have a tooth or teeth that are damaged beyond repair, extraction services can be provided. Following an X-ray and oral evaluation, we can determine your specific needs in regard to the repair of your teeth.

You can be confident in our ability to provide the highest quality dental care for all of your restorative dental needs. We want you to be confident in your smile when you leave our office.

Common Reasons For Tooth Extraction

  • Severe tooth decay
  • Advanced gum disease
  • An unrepairable broken tooth
  • Crowded teeth
  • Infection
  • Problematic Wisdom teeth

Post-Extraction Care

After you have had one or more teeth extracted, it is imperative that you take the proper precautions to prevent complications.

  • Take it easy
  • Take pain medication immediately
  • Consume soft food for the first few days
  • Don't rinse for at least 24 hours
