Save Your Natural Teeth

Millions of teeth are saved each year with root canal services. Although this process has a reputation of being painful and uncomfortable, our experienced staff at Wisler Dental can ensure that you have a relaxing and comfortable experience during your dental visits.

Root Canal Process

When the nerve or pulp of a tooth has become infected, a root canal procedure is performed to repair and save the natural tooth. During a root canal procedure, the nerve and pulp are removed, and the inside of the tooth is cleaned and sealed.

With over 30 years of experience, our team at Wisler Dental can guarantee that your dental services are completed with great care and a gentle touch. Our warm and inviting office environment will make you feel like a member of our family.

Is A Root Canal Needed?

  • Darkening of the tooth
  • Swelling and tnderness in surrounding gums
  • Severe toothache
  • Pain upon chewing
  • Prolonged sensitvity to hot or cold

Benefits Of Saving The Natural Tooth

  • Protects other teeth from excessive wear
  • Normal biting force and sensation
  • Efficient chewing
  • Natural appearance
