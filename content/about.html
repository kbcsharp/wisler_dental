Meet Our Dentist and Staff

Our Dentist

Dr. Craig A. Wisler is a full-time practicing dentist as well as an active Lieutenant Colonel in the Air Force Reserves. A 1984 graduate of Indiana University School Of Dentistry, Dr. Wisler founded Wisler Dental to help people find quality, affordable dental care.


Dr. Wisler is a member of the following professional organizations:

  • Academy of General Dentistry
  • American Dental Association
  • Indiana Dental Association
  • Indiana District Dental Society

"I want to give the patient a great value for a great service provided. There is an art and science to Dentistry and it is all about how you treat people. The science side is the actual act of Dentistry. I am an active dentist and have treated people since 1984 so the science side comes easy. The art side of treating people is where God has truly blessed me. I know how to genuinely make the patient feel comfortable and I know the meaning of being compassionate." - Dr. Wisler
