Meet Our Staff

At Wisler Dental, we take great pride in taking care of each patient's specialized needs while providing a relaxing environment. We are committed to using only the highest quality materials for your dental services; the gold crowns are a high gold content and the porcelain crowns are high quality porcelain.

Our entire team of professionals loves what they do and are here to make your dental visit go as smooth as possible.

Everyone on our team is committed to being a life-long learner, always looking for ways to improve the patient experience. Visiting the dentist does not have to be an unpleasant experiance. We'll do all we can to make sure your experience with us is an enjoyable one!

Dr. Craig Wisler - Dentist

Dr. Craig A. Wisler is a full-time practicing dentist as well as an active Lieutenant Colonel in the Air Force Reserves. A 1984 graduate of Indiana University School Of Dentistry, Dr. Wisler founded Wisler Dental to help people find quality, affordable dental care.

Dr. Wisler is a member of the following professional organizations:

  • Academy of General Dentistry
  • American Dental Association
  • Indiana Dental Association
  • Indiana District Dental Society
