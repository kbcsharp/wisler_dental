Wisler Dental Membership Plan

We know the cost of dental care is expensive. We also know that it is a difficult expense to budget. In an effort to make oral health a regular and affordable option; Dr. Wisler is excited to introduce you to our Dental Membership Plan! This is NOT an insurance program, nor is it associated with any insurance company or other dental discount programs.

FOR LESS THAN A DOLLAR A DAY, YOU CAN BE BACK ON A PATH TO GOOD ORAL HEALTH!

This membership plan has:

No Annual Maximums

No Waiting Periods

No Annual Deductibles

No Procedure Restrictions

No Claim Forms


We have established a program that allows uninsured patients to pay less out-of-pocket fees than they normally would while paying as they go. In addition, our plan gives Members the opportunity of similar savings on all your dental care needs!


Here's How It Works


Enroll in the Wisler Dental Membership Plan. Membership plans are a one year commitment.

Only $339 per person. Pay annually for Maximum Savings, or simply pay $30 per person, per month. The monthly payment will be automatically withdrawn and will renew annually to give you uninterrupted care.

Each Member gains the benefit of top of the line dental care as shown below, and will receive exceptional savings along the way.


Annual Wisler Dental Membership Plan Includes:

  • 2 Periodic Exams
  • 2 Oral Cancer Screenings
  • 2 Basic Cleanings
  • 2 Periodontal Screenings
  • 1 Fluoride Treatment (13 & under only)
  • 1 Set of 4 Bitewing X-rays

PLUS.... Plan Members get additional discounts on all other services, as listed below:

  • 15% Savings on other Diagnostic, Preventive and Basic Restorative Care
  • 15% Savings on Periodontal & Endodontic Services and Oral Surgery
  • 10% Savings on Major Restorative Care: Crowns, Bridges, Partials, Dentures and Implant Services
  • 10% Savings on In-Home Teeth Whitening Kits and Other Hygiene Related Health Aids
  • * Additional 2% Savings for CASH payments.

Additional Benefits with Continued Membership Included:

1 Comprehensive Exam: every 3 years, as needed

1 Full Mouth or Panoramic X-Ray: every 3 years, as needed


Our Goal: Make Dental Care An Affordable Option For Every Person!


Everyone knows that Dental Health is important, and most everyone knows what can happen when regular dental checkups do not occur. However, dental insurance is not always affordable, and paying a large lump sum at the time of appointment is difficult for many. Membership with our Dental Membership Plan simplifies it into a simple annual fee for maximum savings, or it can be paid into a reasonable monthly fee that can be easily put into a budget.

We Are Not Kidding!!

We’ve gone the extra mile to develop this plan, because we care about the oral health of this community. We know what happens when dental care gets neglected, and we don’t want that to happen to you!

FAQ


Who is Eligible?

Anyone without dental insurance is eligible!

How do I sign up?

It's easy! Just click this button or ask our office staff to help you enroll. if you're already coming in for an appointment, let us know if you would like to become a member and start saving now.

How do I pay for the Dental Membership Plan?

You can pay for this plan when you enroll online using VISA, MASTERCARD, DISCOVER AND AMERICAN EXPRESS Cards. You can also enroll in automatic payment deductions through your bank using ACH.

Is this a Dental Insurance Program?

This is not an insurance program. Our Dental Membership Plan is not associated with any insurance company or discount program.

Will my participation require a contract?

Yes. Enrollment will require a contract. To avoid interruption in your care, Membership will renew automatically, on your anniversary date, unless otherwise specified by the Member.

How do I pay for the Dental Membership Plan?

You can pay for the Plan while enrolling online using: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover Cards. You can also enroll in automatic payment deductions through your bank using ACH. Care Credit payments are welcome at a Reduced Discount Percentage, but cannot be used to purchase our Dental Membership Plan.

How long before my Savings begin?

There is not waiting! You can take advantage of our Dental Membership Plan savings as soon as you become a member.

Can I use this Plan at other dental offices?

This Plan is administered and honored at Wisler Dental, LLC only, and is not valid at any other dental office. Referrals to Specialists, and their fees, are not covered under this Plan.

Can someone else use my Dental Membership Plan for dental services?

Unfortunately, Memberships are not transferable to another person. Persons not covered under our Dental Membership Plan are not eligible for the benefits provided to Members. Non- Members will be billed at our Fee-For-Service rates.

Am I limited in the services I can receive?

There is NO maximum on the number of services you can receive. Therefore, your savings are unlimited!

What if I don't use all my Plan benefits this year; can they be rolled over to next year?

There are no rollover benefits for unused services under this Plan.

I just had dental care services completed or started last week, will I get a discount credit for those services?

Unfortunately, any dental procedures that were completed or started before you joined the Dental Membership Plan, are excluded.

Does Wisler Dental, LLC offer payment arrangements?

Currently, we do not offer payment arrangements. Member’s payment for services are due, and payable, at the time services are rendered. Members that come unprepared to pay at the time of service will be billed at our Fee-For-Service Rates.

Is this Dental Membership Plan available to businesses?

Absolutely! In fact, employers can save thousands each year on dental care costs by taking advantage of our Dental Membership Plan. This Plan eliminates eligibility issues, wait times, claim forms, reimbursement delays, and high administration costs saving time and money.

What if I decide to cancel my Membership?

While it would be very unfortunate for you to not take complete advantage of our Dental Membership Plan, and the valuable savings we offer. You can cancel your Membership. However, Cancellation and refund restriction apply.
