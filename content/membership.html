Wisler Dental Membership Plan

NO INSURANCE... NO WORRIES

This is not a Dental Insurance Program and should not be construed as one!

FOR LESS THAN A DOLLAR A DAY, YOU CAN BE BACK ON A PATH TO GOOD ORAL HEALTH!

All too often we hear of patients and friends who are neglecting their oral health because they don't have insurance. Wisler Dental now offers a Dental Membership Plan so you can get the care you need!

Our In-Office Dental Membership Plan is for both new and existing patients. We understand traditional insurance premiums can be expensive and, in most cases, benefits are extremely limited. The good news is, as a member of our Dental Membership Plan, you can help make taking care of your smile affordable for you and your whole family. And you can get back on the road to good dental oral health and receive valuable discounts along the way.


Advantages

The many advantages of our annual Dental Membership Plan include:
  • 2 Exams (1 Comprehensive Exam every three years with continued membership)
  • 2 Basic Cleanings
  • 1 Fluoride Treatment (age 13 and under only)
  • 2 Oral Cancer Screenings
  • 2 Periodontal Screenings
  • 1 Set of 4-Bitewing X-rays
  • 1 Full Mouth or Pano X-Ray (1 every three years with continued membership
  • 15% Off other Diagnostic, Preventive and Basic Restorative Care
  • 15% Off Periodontal Care, Oral Surgery and Root Canals
  • 15% Off In-Home Teeth Whitening Kits and other Hygiene related aids
  • 10% Off Major Care (Crowns, Bridges, Partials, Dentures and Implant Servcies
  • * Additional 2% Savings for CASH payments.

FAQ


How do I sign up?

It's easy! Just click this button or ask our office staff to help you enroll. if you're already coming in for an appointment, let us know if you would like to become a member and start saving now.

How do I pay for the Dental Membership Plan?

You can pay for this plan when you enroll online using VISA, MASTERCARD, DISCOVER AND AMERICAN EXPRESS Cards. You can also enroll in automatic payment deductions through your bank using ACH.

How long before my plans discounts begin?

There is no wait! You can take advantage of our Dental Membership plan discounts as soon as you become a member. Best of all...there is no maximum on the number of services you can have performed, so your savings are unlimited!

Is this Dental Membership Plan available to businesses?

Absolutely! In fact, employers can save thousands each year on dental health care costs by taking advantages of our Dental Membership Plan too. This Dental Membership Plan eliminates eligibility hassles, wait times, claim forms, reimbursement delays, and high administrative costs saving companies money and times.


Plan Details, Limitations and Exclusions


* Enrollment will require a contract. Members underneath this plan will be automatically re-enrolled annually on your membership anniversary date.

* This Dental Membership Plan is administered and honored at Wisler Dental LLC only and is not valid at any other dentist office. Referrals to specialists and their fees are not covered underneath this plan

* There are no rollover benefits for unused services under this Dental Membership Plan.

* Memberships are not transferrable to another person. Persons not covered underneath a Dental Membership Plan are not eligible for any discounts as provided underneath this plan.

* Any dental procedures in progress or performed before a members eligibility begins are excluded.

* Member's payment for services under this plan are due at the time services are rendered.

* Care Credit Payments are welcome at a Reduced Discount Percentage and cannot be used to purchase the Dental Membership Plan.
