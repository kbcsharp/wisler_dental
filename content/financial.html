Financial Information

At Wisler Dental, we understand that financial concerns about dental treatment are a very real consideration for our patients. Particularly for those patients who are uninsured. We want to ensure that all of our patients receive the best possible dental care.

In addition to accepting limited insurance companies, we provide several different payment options. We don't want you to put off getting any necessary dental treatment! In fact, delaying necessary dental work can be far more detrimental to not only your oral health but also your pocketbook.

If you have any questions, please be sure to contact our office for more information on payment options.

Insurance

We currently accept limited insurance companies.

We are out-of-network with all other insurance plans. However, we will bill other insurance companies and do what we can to get the best reimbursement for our patients.

If you have any questions about using your insurance with us, please contact our office!

Third-Party Financing

As a service to our patients, we are partnered with CareCredit. CareCredit is the nation's leading patient payment program. With CareCredit, you can finance 100 percent of your dental care and thre are no upfront costs, no annual fees, and no pre-payment penalties. You can begin your treatment today and conveniently pay with low, monthly payment.

CareCredit can be used by the whole family for ongoing treatment without having to reapply. Please see the CareCredit website for more information.

Other Payment Options

  • Check
  • Visa and MasterCard
